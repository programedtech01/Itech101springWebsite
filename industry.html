Industry Overview

The electrical industry provides the essential infrastructure for power generation and distribution. It is currently evolving through the integration of renewable energy and smart-grid technologies.

Career Interest: Electrical Engineer

Electrical Engineers design and maintain electrical systems. I am interested in this role because it combines technical problem-solving with the ability to build sustainable energy solutions.

Ethical Issue: Grid Reliability vs. Cost

An ethical dilemma arises when companies must choose between expensive infrastructure upgrades for safety and maintaining lower costs for shareholders. Rushing inspections or using cheaper materials can lead to dangerous outages.

Personal Values & Response

Using my value of Responsibility, I would prioritize public safety over cutting corners. My value of Integrity ensures that I would report any safety violations I encounter, even if it is unpopular with management.

Cybersecurity Issues

Modern electrical grids are vulnerable to cyber-attacks on Industrial Control Systems (ICS). Hackers could potentially gain access to the grid to cause blackouts or physical damage to equipment.

Cybersecurity Best Practices

Key practices include "Air-gapping" critical control systems from the public internet and requiring Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for all technicians accessing grid software.
